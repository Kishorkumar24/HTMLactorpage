vijay

Vijay , born: June 22 , 1974 ; birth name: Joseph Vijay [1] ) is a Tamil film actor. In the beginning, he met his father S. A. He used to act in films directed by Chandrasekhar . He retained his position after about 10 films. He is currently seen as one of the leading actors in the Tamil film industry. Vijay's fans call him "Thalapati". He has fans in China , [2] Japan , [3] the United Kingdom [4] and France , including the Indian diaspora . [5] His films have been released on five continents and eighty countries.
